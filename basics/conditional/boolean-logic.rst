Boolean Logic
=============


Positive and Negative Values
----------------------------
Negative values:

    * empty ``bool()`` or ``False``
    * empty ``int()`` or ``0``
    * empty ``float()`` or ``0.0``
    * empty ``complex()`` or ``0+0j`` or ``0.0+0.0j``
    * empty ``str()`` or ``''``
    * empty ``tuple()`` or ``()``
    * empty ``list()`` or ``[]``
    * empty ``set()``
    * empty ``frozenset()``
    * empty ``dict()`` or ``{}``
    * ``None``

Positive values:

    * any other
